# Pet Feeder IoT

The purpose of this project is to develop a Smart Pet Feeder IoT system capable of automatically managing food and water supplies for pets while allowing owners to monitor and control the system remotely through a mobile application.

The project aims to improve pet care convenience, reduce manual intervention, and ensure pets receive adequate food and water even when owners are away from home. By integrating IoT technologies, the system provides real-time monitoring, automation, and future support for intelligent data analysis.

## Goal

The objectives of this project are:

To automatically dispense food when the food level falls below a predefined threshold.
To automatically refill water when the water level becomes insufficient.
To continuously monitor food and water conditions using sensors.
To display real-time system information on an LCD screen.
To enable remote monitoring and control through the Blynk mobile application.
To provide notifications when food or water levels require attention.
To collect and store consumption data for future analysis.
To establish a foundation for AI-based behavior analysis, anomaly detection, and consumption forecasting.
To improve the overall reliability, efficiency, and user experience of pet care management.
